About Me

My journey into Cybersecurity began with curiosity, the kind that makes you wonder what really happens behind the screen when someone tries to break in. That curiosity soon turned into purpose. Today, I work as a Cyber Security Analyst in a live Cyber Security Operations Center (CSOC), where every alert tells a story of patterns, persistence, and protection.

Each day, I dive deep into threat detection, vulnerability management, and real-time alert triage, tracing digital footprints and connecting dots to uncover what others might overlook. What excites me most is the chase, that critical moment where analysis meets intuition and an incident turns into insight.

I hold a Bachelor of Networking (Cybersecurity major) from the Melbourne Institute of Technology, and I continue to strengthen my craft through hands-on simulations and continuous learning.

This site captures my journey, a collection of detection stories while exploring different sectors of this diverse field, blue team insights, and case studies that reflect my passion for defending, investigating, and evolving in the ever-changing world of Cybersecurity.
